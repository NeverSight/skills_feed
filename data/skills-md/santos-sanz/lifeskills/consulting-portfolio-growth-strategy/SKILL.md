# Portfolio and Growth Strategy

Use $ARGUMENTS as initial context.

## When to use this skill
- Portfolio reviews and resource allocation.
- Evaluating business units or product lines.
- Defining growth moves and sequencing.

## How to use this skill
1. Define portfolio units, metrics, and time horizon.
2. Choose the right framework for the decision.
3. Score each unit for attractiveness and competitive position.
4. Map units and identify invest, hold, harvest, or exit.
5. Propose growth moves and sequencing.
6. Call out risks, dependencies, and next analyses.
7. If information is missing, ask targeted questions.

## Resources
- `references/portfolio-frameworks.md` - Framework rules and axes.
- `references/growth-levers.md` - Growth move options.
- `templates/portfolio-growth-plan.md` - Final structure.
- `examples/portfolio-growth-example.md` - Sample output.

## Output guidelines
- Provide a short resource allocation summary.
- Make assumptions and scoring criteria explicit.
- Respond in English.

## Missing information to ask for
- Business units and key metrics.
- Strategic objectives and constraints.
- Data availability and time horizon.
