YouTube video smart clipping tool. Downloads video and subtitles, AI analyzes to generate fine chapters (minute-level), users select segments for automatic clipping, translates subtitles into Chinese and English, burns subtitles into the video, and generates a summary. Use case: when users need to clip YouTube videos, create short clips, or produce bilingual subtitle versions. Keywords: video clipping, YouTube, subtitle translation, bilingual subtitles, video download, clip video
