YouTube video smart clipping tool. Downloads video and subtitles, AI analyzes to generate fine-grained chapters (minute-level), users select segments for automatic clipping, translates subtitles into Chinese-English bilingual, burns subtitles into the video, and generates summary copy. Use case: when users need to clip YouTube videos, create short clips, or produce bilingual subtitle versions. Keywords: video editing, YouTube, subtitle translation, bilingual subtitles, video download, clip video
