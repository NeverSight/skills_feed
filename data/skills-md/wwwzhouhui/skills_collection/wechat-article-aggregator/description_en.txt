WeChat Official Account Article Aggregator. Batch-fetch the latest article list of specified bloggers via the mptext.top API, download article content and parse it into Markdown/HTML/plain text. Support retrieval by Official Account name or fakeid, with 8 preset popular AI-tech accounts. Use cases: fetch latest articles from a given account, batch-scrape multiple bloggers, aggregate articles for reading, collect tech news. Keywords: Official Account article fetching, WeChat article scraping, account aggregation, article collection, mptext API.
