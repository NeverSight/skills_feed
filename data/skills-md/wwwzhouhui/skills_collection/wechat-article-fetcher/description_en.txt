Get and parse WeChat Official Account articles. Extract title, author, account name, body text, images, and metadata from WeChat article links. Use when the user provides a WeChat article link (mp.weixin.qq.com/s/...) and wants to read, extract, download, or convert the content. Applicable scenarios include obtaining/downloading WeChat articles, extracting text or metadata, converting to Markdown, or saving articles with images locally. Keywords: WeChat Official Account, article retrieval, article scraping, article download.
