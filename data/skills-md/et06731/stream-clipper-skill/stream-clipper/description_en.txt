Live Clip Smart Editor. Core rule: extract subtitles before clipping! Two modes:  
1) Live Recording: real-time capture, auto-split every 30 min to prevent disk overflow, record danmaku simultaneously;  
2) VOD Clipping: download replays (Bilibili/YouTube/etc.), must extract subtitles + danmaku, smart clip based on subtitle content and danmaku density, streamer-style templates, auto-generate suspenseful/quote-based titles and descriptions from actual dialogue, one-click upload to platforms.  
Use cases: live capture, clip creation, VTuber editing, highlight extraction, batch upload.  
Keywords: live clip, subtitle extraction, danmaku analysis, VTuber, smart edit, auto upload
