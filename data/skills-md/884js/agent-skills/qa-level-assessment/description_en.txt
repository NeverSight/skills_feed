Skill to determine QA test level. Combines story descriptions, code diffs, and dialogue to comprehensively assess risk and propose the appropriate QA level.
Use in the following situations:
- When you want to determine the QA level for a PR
- When you need to decide whether testing or double-checking is necessary
- When setting the level for a test perspective review
Can also be invoked with "QA judgment", "test level", "risk assessment", etc.
