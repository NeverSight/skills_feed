Skill to determine QA test level. It comprehensively assesses risk by combining story descriptions, code diffs, and dialogue, then proposes an appropriate QA level. Use in the following situations:
- When you want to determine the QA level of a PR
- When you need to decide whether a tester or double-check is required
- When setting the level for a test viewpoint review
Can also be invoked with "QA judgment", "test level", "risk assessment", etc.
