CRISP Reading — AI deep-reading companion. Comprehend · Review · Internalize · Synthesize · Practice. Integrates Adler’s analytical reading, Fan Deng TIPS book-dissection, RIA “book-dismantling,” Zettelkasten, Feynman technique, Self-Explanation, Steel-Manning. Analyzes books and outputs interactive HTML reading reports. Use when: (1) user says “read this book,” “analyze this book,” “help me read,” “is this book worth reading,” “analyze this book,” “book review,” “book summary,” “reading notes,” “what’s this book about,” “deep reading”; (2) requests book evaluation, reading notes, reading report; (3) mentions CRISP, CRISP Reading, deep reading. Not for: pure document summarization (“summarize this” with no reading intent), systematic literature reviews of academic papers, speed-reading training.
