A comprehensive, structured feature-development workflow with dedicated stages for codebase exploration, architecture design, quality review, and implementation. Use it when building new features that require: (1) understanding the existing codebase before making changes, (2) clarifying ambiguous requirements through systematic questioning, (3) designing thoughtful architecture before implementation, and (4) ensuring quality through multi-dimensional code reviews. Ideal for features that span multiple files, require architectural decisions, involve complex integrations, or have somewhat unclear requirements.
