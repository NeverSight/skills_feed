All-in-One Deep Reading Tool. Designed for deep digestion of books/long articles/research papers/theses. Use when the user wants to deeply digest a book/long article/research paper/thesis and build a knowledge network, producing structured notes and atomic notes. Integrates Mortimer Adler (structure), Feynman (explanation), Luhmann (network), Pragmatist (tooling), Critics (debate); enforces high-fidelity case retention and actionable tool extraction. Keywords: deep reading, structured notes, deep learning, Luhmann.
