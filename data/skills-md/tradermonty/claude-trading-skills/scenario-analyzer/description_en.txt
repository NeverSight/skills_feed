The skill to analyze an 18-month scenario using a news headline as input.  
Primary analysis is run by the scenario-analyst agent,  
with a second opinion from the strategy-reviewer agent.  
Generates a comprehensive report in Japanese covering primary, secondary, and tertiary impacts, recommended stocks, and reviews.  
Usage example: /scenario-analyzer "Fed raises rates by 50bp"  
Triggers: news analysis, scenario analysis, 18-month outlook, medium- to long-term investment strategy
