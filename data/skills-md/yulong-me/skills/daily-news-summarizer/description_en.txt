Automatically summarize daily headlines from multiple news websites using browser automation to access and read news content. Applicable for user requests like "summarize today's news," "get today's headlines," or "generate a news digest," as well as questions such as "What are the important news today?" or "Show me today's top stories from news sites." Supports Chinese news sites (Sina, NetEase, Tencent, etc.) and international news sites (BBC, Reuters, etc.), producing detailed summary reports in Markdown format.
