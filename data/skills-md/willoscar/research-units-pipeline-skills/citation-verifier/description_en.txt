Generate and verify BibTeX entries from paper notes, writing `citations/ref.bib` and `citations/verified.jsonl`.  
**Trigger**: citation, BibTeX, ref.bib, verified.jsonl, references, 引用, 参考文献.  
**Use when**: `papers/paper_notes.jsonl` already exists and you need traceable citations (each with url/date/title verification record) for prose/LaTeX.  
**Skip if**: no paper notes yet (or the current output doesn’t need citations/references).  
**Network**: automatic verification usually requires network; if offline, record first, then mark needs manual verification.  
**Guardrail**: every BibTeX entry must have a matching `citations/verified.jsonl` record; prose can only use citation keys already in `citations/ref.bib`.
