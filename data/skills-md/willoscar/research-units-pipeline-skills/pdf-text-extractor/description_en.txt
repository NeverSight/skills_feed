Download PDFs (when available) and extract plain text to support full-text evidence, writing `papers/fulltext_index.jsonl` and `papers/fulltext/*.txt`.
**Trigger**: PDF download, fulltext, extract text, papers/pdfs, full-text extraction, download PDF.
**Use when**: `queries.md` sets `evidence_mode: fulltext` (or you explicitly need full-text evidence) and you want to provide stronger evidence for paper notes/claims.
**Skip if**: `evidence_mode: abstract` (default); or you don’t want to perform download/extraction (cost/permissions/time).
**Network**: fulltext download usually requires network (unless you manually provide PDF cache in `papers/pdfs/`).
**Guardrail**: cache downloads to `papers/pdfs/`; by default do not overwrite existing extracted text (unless explicitly requested to re-extract).
