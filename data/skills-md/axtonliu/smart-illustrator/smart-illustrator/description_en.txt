Smart illustration and PPT infographic generator. Supports three modes: (1) Article illustration mode – analyzes article content and generates illustrations; (2) PPT/Slides mode – batch-generates infographics; (3) Cover mode – generates cover images. All modes produce images by default; `--prompt-only` outputs only the prompt. Trigger words: 配图, 插图, PPT, slides, 封面图, thumbnail, cover.
