Smart image-matching & PPT infographic generator. Supports three modes: (1) Article-illustration mode – analyzes article content and generates illustrations; (2) PPT/Slides mode – generates infographics in batches; (3) Cover mode – generates cover images. All modes output images by default; use `--prompt-only` to output only the prompt. Trigger words: illustration, image, PPT, slides, cover image, thumbnail, cover.
