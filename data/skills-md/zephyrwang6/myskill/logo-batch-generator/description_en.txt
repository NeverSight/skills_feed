Batch-generate brand logos. Two entry points: (1) After the user describes the brand/purpose, ten logo variants are generated in bulk; (2) After the user uploads a reference image, it is analyzed and logos are created. Offers seven mainstream style choices. Uses the Gemini API to generate and saves to the Obsidian images folder. Trigger phrases: “generate logo”, “design logo”, “make a logo”, “batch logos”, “design me an emblem”.
