Topic Selection System Master Agent. Coordinates hot-spot collection, topic generation, and topic review, supporting iteration until a qualified topic is produced. Triggers: (1) "Start Today's Topics" launches the full workflow; (2) "Today's AI Hot Spots" collects hot spots only, no topic generation; (3) "I Have a Topic" enters single-topic analysis; (4) "Recommend Some Good Topics" outputs recommendations directly. Outputs are saved to the Obsidian topic vault.
