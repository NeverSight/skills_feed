Feishu Knowledge Base document management tool. Supports viewing the knowledge base directory structure, creating and saving documents to the Feishu Knowledge Base. Triggered when the user says "save to Feishu", "write to Feishu Knowledge Base", "publish to Feishu", "view Feishu directory", or "Feishu Knowledge Base structure". Suitable for syncing Obsidian documents to the Feishu Knowledge Base or creating new documents in Feishu.
