A one-stop tool for video script analysis. Download online videos (Bilibili/YouTube/Douyin, etc.), transcribe Chinese speech with FunASR Nano, auto-correct the transcript, and perform three-dimensional analysis (TextContent/Viral/Brainstorming). Use case: when users need to analyze short-video scripts, extract video content, or learn viral copywriting techniques. Keywords: video analysis, script analysis, speech-to-text, FunASR, Whisper, viral analysis, video download
