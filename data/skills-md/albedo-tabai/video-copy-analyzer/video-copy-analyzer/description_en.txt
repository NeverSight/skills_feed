One-stop tool for video copy analysis. Download online videos (Bilibili/YouTube/TikTok, etc.), transcribe Chinese speech with FunASR Nano, auto-correct the transcript, and perform a three-dimensional comprehensive analysis (TextContent/Viral/Brainstorming).  
Use case: When users need to analyze short-video copy, extract video content, or learn viral copy techniques.  
Keywords: video analysis, copy analysis, speech-to-text, FunASR, Whisper, viral analysis, video download
