# Tailored Resume Generator

This skill analyzes job postings and customizes your resume to highlight the most relevant experience, skills, and achievements for each specific position.

## When to Use This Skill

- Applying for a specific job position
- Transitioning to a new career
- Customizing for different industries
- Creating multiple resume versions
- Optimizing for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Preparing for job applications

## What This Skill Does

1. **Extracts Requirements**: Analyzes job descriptions for key requirements
2. **Identifies Priorities**: Determines what employers value most
3. **Reorganizes Content**: Highlights relevant experience first
4. **Incorporates Keywords**: Adds searchable terms naturally
5. **Formats Professionally**: Creates clean, ATS-friendly layouts

## How to Use

### Basic Usage
```
Tailor my resume for this job posting:
[paste job description]

My background:
[paste current resume or experience summary]
```

### With Specific Focus
```
Customize my resume for this Senior Developer role.
Emphasize my Python experience and team leadership.
[paste job description]
```

### Career Transition
```
I'm transitioning from marketing to product management.
Help me reframe my experience for this PM role:
[paste job description]
```

## The 10-Step Process

### 1. Information Gathering
- Collect job posting details
- Review your background and experience
- Understand the target company

### 2. Requirements Analysis
- Extract hard requirements (must-have)
- Identify soft requirements (nice-to-have)
- Prioritize by importance

### 3. Experience Mapping
- Match your experience to requirements
- Identify transferable skills
- Note gaps to address

### 4. Resume Structure
```
Contact Information
Professional Summary (tailored)
Key Skills (keyword-optimized)
Professional Experience (reordered by relevance)
Education
Certifications (if relevant)
```

### 5. ATS Optimization
- Use exact keywords from job posting
- Standard section headings
- Clean formatting (no tables/graphics)
- PDF or Word format

### 6. Professional Presentation
- Consistent formatting
- Action verbs and metrics
- Clear hierarchy
- Appropriate length

### 7. Strategic Recommendations
- Highlight strengths
- Address gaps strategically
- Suggest additional skills to add

### 8. Iterative Refinement
- Review and adjust based on feedback
- Multiple versions if needed

### 9. Best Practices
- All claims must be truthful
- Quantify achievements when possible
- Use industry terminology
- Keep it focused and concise

### 10. Special Considerations
- **Career changers**: Emphasize transferable skills
- **Executives**: Focus on strategic impact
- **Technical roles**: Include relevant tech stack
- **Creative roles**: Balance creativity with clarity

## Additional Support

### Interview Preparation

- Prepare STAR stories for key achievements
- Address potential gap questions

### Cover Letter Suggestions
- Opening hook ideas
- Key points to emphasize
- Closing call-to-action

### Gap Analysis
- Skills to develop
- Certifications to pursue
- Experience to gain

## Best Practices

1. **Be Truthful**: Never fabricate experience
2. **Quantify Impact**: Use numbers and metrics
3. **Match Keywords**: Mirror the job posting language
4. **Stay Focused**: One page for <10 years, two max for senior
5. **Proofread**: Zero typos or errors
6. **Update LinkedIn**: Keep it consistent
