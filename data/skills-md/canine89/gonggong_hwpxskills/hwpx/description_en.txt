Skill to create, read, edit, and template-substitute HWPX documents (.hwpx files). Whenever keywords such as 'Hangul document', 'hwpx', 'HWPX', 'Hangul file', 'make a .hwpx file', 'create HWP document', 'report', 'official letter', 'draft', 'write in Hangul' appear, this skill must be used. It handles Hancom's HWPX format (KS X 6101/OWPML-based, ZIP+XML structure) via the python-hwpx library. When a report template is needed, use the reference templates in the assets/ folder. For regular Word (.docx) documents, use the docx skill.
