YouTube video smart clipping tool. Downloads video and subtitles, AI analyzes to generate fine-grained chapters (minute-level), users select segments for automatic clipping, translates subtitles into Chinese-English bilingual, burns subtitles into video, and generates summary copy.

Usage scenario: When users need to clip YouTube videos, generate short video segments, or create bilingual subtitle versions.

Keywords: video clipping, YouTube, subtitle translation, bilingual subtitles, video download, clip video
