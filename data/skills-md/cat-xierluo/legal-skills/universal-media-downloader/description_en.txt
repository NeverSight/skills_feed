After entering links from various video sites or podcast platforms, automatically download the corresponding media files and deliver them to the user. Prioritize using yt-dlp for common video sites like Douyin, Bilibili, and YouTube; also works for podcast platforms that expose direct audio URLs (e.g., Xiaoyu episode links). When encountering 403/login/age or region restrictions, retry with cookies.txt; for platforms that may have DRM/encryption or terms limitations (e.g., some Spotify content), remind users to download only content they have the right to save, and suggest using official offline/export channels or providing the original RSS/direct link when download is not possible. Note: Douyin photo-text notes are not currently supported for automatic download and must be handled manually.
