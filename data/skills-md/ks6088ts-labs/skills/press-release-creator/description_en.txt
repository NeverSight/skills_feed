A skill to create press releases and FAQs based on Amazon’s Working Backwards method. At the planning stage of a new service or feature, it articulates value from the customer’s perspective and sets the direction for development. Triggered by requests such as “create a press release,” “write a PR/FAQ,” “plan with Working Backwards,” or “design a customer-centric service.” Useful for planning and requirements definition of IT services, products, and new features.
